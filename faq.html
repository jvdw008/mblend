Why will my project not run?

Are you sure you don't have any build errors?

Are you sure your main filename is the same as the project name? (This is a Monkey-X requirement)

How can I get all my files?

If you are a paid user, you are able to Zip up your projects for download. As a free user, you can copy and paste your code into another editor.

The font-size is too large/small. How can I change this?

As a paid user, you have access to various options, including changing the font-size for the editor.

How do I share my build folder?

Build your project, then click the Launch button. Grab the resultant URL from your browser address bar.

The build is running slowly in my browser

That's because it's using your device's own resources. If you have a slow phone or tablet, running web-based games on it may not run as fast as you expected.

Why when I double click on a keyword does no help appear?

If the keyword exists it will show an inline help box which displays the definition or syntax. If it doesn't try press F1 to go to the full Monkey-X documentation.

There are updates to the editor but I don't see them?

Most likely it's your browser cache. You will have to empty/clear your browser cache and do a hard refresh to see the updates. Follow this guide.

I have discovered a problem or bug. How do I contact you to get it resolved?

Visit our main website at https://mblend.domain/ and at the bottom select the "Contact us" link.
Please include as much information as you can, ie. Browser used, Operating system, Device type used, what you did and how we can replicate it to fix it.
